Welcome to the Procyon Sector Navigation Console

The Procyon Sector is a region of space that has been colonized by the Hegemony for over a century. This interactive map allows you to explore the various systems, planets, and notable locations within the sector.

About the Data

The Procyon sector and all other Scum and Villiany data is owned by Evil Hat Productions. This application is not affiliated with Evil Hat Productions and was made for fun

Key Features

  • • Interactive map of the entire Procyon Sector
  • • Detailed information about systems and planets
  • • Notable locations and figures
  • • Responsive design for both desktop and mobile devices

Navigation

Use the following controls to navigate the map:

  • • Click and drag to pan the map
  • • Use the mouse wheel or pinch gestures to zoom
  • • Double-click to zoom in at a specific point
  • • Click on any highlighted area to view detailed information
