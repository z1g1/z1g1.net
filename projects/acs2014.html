Social Media for Surgeons (#SM4S) was presented at the ACS 2014 confrence in San Francisco, CA. The focus of this hands-on course is how surgeons can use health care social media (#HCSM) (Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn, Google+ , etc.) in their daily lives to enhance patient care and outcomes, medical student (#UME) and resident (#GME) education, career development (#CME) and personal networking.
